# Business Associate Agreement — Provider Beta

**Covered Entity:** Provider Beta (a rural community hospital)
**Business Associate:** ClearFlow Health Network ("ClearFlow")
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2024
**Services:** Claims pricing and payment routing

This Agreement governs ClearFlow's Use and Disclosure of PHI under HIPAA, the HITECH
Act, and 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164. Provider Beta is a Critical Access participant with
limited internal compliance staff and relies on ClearFlow to act protectively.

## 1. Definitions

As set forth in 45 CFR §§ 160.103 and 164.501.

## 2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

2.1 ClearFlow may Use or Disclose PHI solely as necessary to perform the Services.

2.2 ClearFlow shall apply the minimum necessary standard and shall not Disclose PHI to
any payer without Provider Beta's prior written authorization.

## 3. Safeguards

ClearFlow shall maintain Security Rule safeguards (45 CFR §§ 164.308–312).

## 4. Breach Notification

ClearFlow shall notify Provider Beta of a Breach of Unsecured PHI without unreasonable
delay and **no later than sixty (60) calendar days** after Discovery (the HIPAA outer
limit, 45 CFR § 164.410).

## 5. Disclosures Required by Law; Legal Process

5.1 Upon receipt of a subpoena, court order, or compulsory legal process seeking Provider
Beta's PHI, ClearFlow shall provide Provider Beta **written notice within five (5)
business days** of receipt.

5.2 ClearFlow shall, **on Provider Beta's behalf and without requiring Provider Beta to
act first, object to the legal process** and assert all available privileges and
protections, withholding disclosure until the objection is resolved to the extent
legally permissible. Provider Beta delegates this protective duty to ClearFlow given its
limited legal resources.

## 6. Subcontractors

ClearFlow shall **not** engage any subcontractor (including the Nimbus Patient Engagement
Platform) to handle Provider Beta PHI **without Provider Beta's written consent**.

## 7. Term and Termination

Terminates on return/destruction of PHI or material breach uncured within thirty (30)
days. Sections 4 and 5 survive.

## 8. Indemnification

ClearFlow shall indemnify Provider Beta for losses arising from ClearFlow's breach,
including any unauthorized subcontractor disclosure.
