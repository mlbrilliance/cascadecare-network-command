# Business Associate Agreement — Provider Epsilon

**Covered Entity:** Provider Epsilon (a children's hospital)
**Business Associate:** ClearFlow Health Network ("ClearFlow")
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Services:** Claims pricing and payment routing for pediatric care

This Agreement governs ClearFlow's Use and Disclosure of PHI under HIPAA, the HITECH
Act, 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164, **and applicable state laws protecting the health
information of minors**, whichever is more protective. ClearFlow acknowledges that
substantially all PHI under this Agreement concerns minors.

## 1. Definitions

As set forth in 45 CFR §§ 160.103 and 164.501. "Minor PHI" means PHI of an individual
under the age of majority under applicable state law, including any specially protected
categories (e.g., behavioral health) recognized by that law.

## 2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

2.1 ClearFlow may Use or Disclose Minor PHI only as necessary to perform the Services and
only to the extent permitted by both HIPAA and applicable state minor-privacy law.

2.2 ClearFlow shall apply the **most protective** standard available and shall treat all
PHI as Minor PHI absent clear evidence to the contrary.

## 3. Safeguards

ClearFlow shall maintain heightened Security Rule safeguards (45 CFR §§ 164.308–312) and
shall segregate Minor PHI from other data stores.

## 4. Breach Notification

ClearFlow shall notify Provider Epsilon of a Breach of Unsecured Minor PHI **within
twenty-four (24) hours** of Discovery, given the heightened sensitivity of pediatric PHI.

## 5. Disclosures Required by Law; Legal Process

5.1 Upon receipt of a subpoena, court order, or compulsory legal process seeking Minor
PHI, ClearFlow shall provide Provider Epsilon **written notice at least fourteen (14)
calendar days before any disclosure**.

5.2 ClearFlow shall **not disclose Minor PHI unless and until a court of competent
jurisdiction has entered a protective order**, or Provider Epsilon has consented in
writing. ClearFlow shall affirmatively **seek a protective order** where one is not
already in place and shall move to quash any demand that does not protect minor identity.

## 6. Subcontractors

ClearFlow shall **not** engage any subcontractor (including the Nimbus Patient Engagement
Platform) to handle Minor PHI under any circumstances.

## 7. Term and Termination

Terminates on return/destruction of PHI or material breach uncured within thirty (30)
days. Sections 4, 5, and 8 survive.

## 8. Indemnification

ClearFlow shall indemnify Provider Epsilon for all losses arising from any unauthorized
Disclosure of Minor PHI, including statutory and state-law penalties.
