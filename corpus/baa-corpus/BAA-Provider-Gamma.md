# Business Associate Agreement — Provider Gamma

**Covered Entity:** Provider Gamma (a multi-state for-profit hospital chain)
**Business Associate:** ClearFlow Health Network ("ClearFlow")
**Effective Date:** October 1, 2024
**Services:** Claims pricing and payment routing across Provider Gamma facilities in multiple states

This Agreement governs ClearFlow's Use and Disclosure of PHI under HIPAA, the HITECH
Act, 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164, **and applicable state privacy laws of each state in which
Provider Gamma operates**, whichever is more stringent.

## 1. Definitions

As set forth in 45 CFR §§ 160.103 and 164.501. "Applicable Law" includes the privacy and
breach-notification statutes of every state in which the affected individuals reside.

## 2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

2.1 ClearFlow may Use or Disclose PHI as necessary to perform the Services and as Required
by Law.

2.2 Where the laws of two or more states conflict as to a permitted Disclosure, ClearFlow
shall apply the **more restrictive** standard.

## 3. Safeguards

ClearFlow shall maintain Security Rule safeguards (45 CFR §§ 164.308–312) and shall
support Provider Gamma's multi-state regulatory reporting.

## 4. Breach Notification

ClearFlow shall notify Provider Gamma of a Breach of Unsecured PHI **within seventy-two
(72) hours** of Discovery, to enable Provider Gamma to meet the shortest applicable
state-law breach deadline.

## 5. Disclosures Required by Law; Legal Process

5.1 ClearFlow **may comply** with a subpoena, court order, or administrative demand that
satisfies 45 CFR § 164.512(e) or (f) without prior notice to Provider Gamma, **provided**
ClearFlow applies the more-restrictive state standard where a state requires patient
notice or a protective order.

5.2 ClearFlow shall promptly inform Provider Gamma of any multi-state legal process that
implicates individuals in more than one state.

## 6. Subcontractors

ClearFlow may engage subcontractors (including the Nimbus Patient Engagement Platform)
under flow-down terms (45 CFR § 164.502(e)(1)(ii)). **ClearFlow indemnifies Provider Gamma
for all subcontractor acts and omissions.**

## 7. Term and Termination

Terminates on return/destruction of PHI or material breach uncured within thirty (30)
days. Sections 4, 5, and 8 survive.

## 8. Indemnification

ClearFlow shall indemnify and hold harmless Provider Gamma for any losses, penalties, or
multi-state regulatory fines arising from ClearFlow's or its subcontractors' breach.
