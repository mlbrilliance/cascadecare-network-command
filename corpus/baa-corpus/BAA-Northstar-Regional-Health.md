# Business Associate Agreement — Northstar Regional Health

**Covered Entity:** Northstar Regional Health (a seven-hospital integrated delivery system)
**Business Associate:** ClearFlow Health Network ("ClearFlow")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Services:** Claims pricing (ClearFlow Pricing Engine) and payment routing (ClearFlow Payment Network)

This Agreement governs ClearFlow's Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information
(PHI) created, received, maintained, or transmitted on behalf of Northstar Regional
Health, in accordance with HIPAA, the HITECH Act, and 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164.

## 1. Definitions

Terms used but not otherwise defined have the meaning set forth in 45 CFR §§ 160.103
and 164.501. "Required by Law" has the meaning at 45 CFR § 164.103.

## 2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

2.1 ClearFlow may Use or Disclose PHI only as necessary to perform the Services and as
permitted by this Agreement.

2.2 ClearFlow shall limit Uses and Disclosures to the **minimum necessary** to
accomplish the intended purpose (45 CFR § 164.502(b)).

2.3 ClearFlow shall not Use or Disclose PHI to any payer, including Apex Health Plan or
SummitBlue Medicare Advantage, **without the prior written authorization** of Northstar
Regional Health, except as Required by Law under Section 5.

## 3. Safeguards

ClearFlow shall implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards under the
Security Rule (45 CFR §§ 164.308, 164.310, 164.312) that reasonably protect the
confidentiality, integrity, and availability of electronic PHI.

## 4. Breach Notification

4.1 ClearFlow shall notify Northstar Regional Health of any Breach of Unsecured PHI
**without unreasonable delay and in no case later than five (5) business days** after
Discovery of the Breach.

4.2 The notice shall include, to the extent known, the identification of each
individual whose PHI was involved, the nature of the Breach, and the remediation taken.

## 5. Disclosures Required by Law; Legal Process

5.1 Where ClearFlow receives a subpoena, court order, civil investigative demand, or
other compulsory legal process seeking PHI of Northstar Regional Health, ClearFlow
shall provide Northstar Regional Health **written notice at least ten (10) calendar
days before any disclosure**, except where such notice is prohibited by law.

5.2 ClearFlow shall, at Northstar Regional Health's request and expense, **object to
the disclosure and cooperate in any effort to quash or limit** the legal process, and
shall withhold disclosure during the pendency of any such effort to the extent legally
permissible.

## 6. Subcontractors

ClearFlow shall not engage any subcontractor (including the Nimbus Patient Engagement
Platform) to create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI without (a) the **prior written
approval** of Northstar Regional Health and (b) a written agreement imposing the same
restrictions and conditions that apply to ClearFlow under this Agreement (flow-down,
45 CFR § 164.502(e)(1)(ii)).

## 7. Term and Termination

This Agreement terminates when all PHI is returned or destroyed, or upon material breach
uncured within thirty (30) days. Obligations surviving termination include Sections 4,
5, and 8.

## 8. Indemnification

Each party shall indemnify the other for losses arising from its own breach of this
Agreement or violation of HIPAA.
